MCR Series Pinouts

CPU | SUPER SOUND I/O

Introduction

The tables below highlight the similarities and differences that can be found within the Midway MCR series of boardsets. Information was gathered either directly from the original manuals or by personal experience and testing.

The following MCR series games do not appear because the information was not available at the time the tables were compiled:

  • Kozmik Krooz'r
  • NFL Football
  • Timber

Mark Jenison and Clay Cowgil have created an excellent resource for the MCR series of games called the MCR FAQ. This document has a lot of interesting information pertaining to the games, history and techical workings behind many of Midway's early to mid-'80s classics.
